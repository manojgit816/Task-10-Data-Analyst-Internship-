
EDA FINDINGS – HOUSE PRICE DATASET

1. Dataset Size: 1200 records with multiple numerical housing features.
2. Missing Values:
   - LotArea and TotalBsmtSF had ~5% missing values.
   - Handled using median imputation.
3. Distribution:
   - SalePrice and GrLivArea showed right-skewed distributions.
4. Outliers:
   - Detected using IQR method.
   - ~6–8% records flagged as outliers.
   - Outliers capped instead of removal to preserve data size.
5. Correlation:
   - Strong positive correlation between SalePrice and:
     - GrLivArea
     - OverallQual
6. Conclusion:
   - Larger living area and higher quality significantly increase house prices.
